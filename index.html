Tournament Dates

Tournament Format

Day 1 (25th January): Each team plays 3 matches. Teams that win at least 2 out of 3 matches will qualify for the finals.

Day 2 (26th January): Republic Day - Finals between qualified teams. Match lots will be announced on 25th January night.

Announcements: Qualified teams list will be announced at 10 PM on 25th January. Match schedule for 26th January will be announced at same time.

Match Timings

All matches will be played from 12:00 PM to 6:00 PM on both days.

Each match duration: Approximately 30-45 minutes

Breaks between matches: 15 minutes
